How We Simplified Your Business

We handle customer queries, shipping updates, and reverse logistics with precision. Our scalable support ensures peak season readiness and reduced delivery TAT. With data-driven insights, we optimize supply chain performance end-to-end.

Order Management & Fulfillment

Streamlining the order lifecycle from confirmation to final delivery.

Order Processing

Automated intake, validation, and assignment of orders to fulfillment centers.

Inventory Coordination

Syncing stock levels across multiple channels and warehouses.

Order Routing

Intelligent routing to nearest/fastest fulfillment center.

Packing & Labeling Instructions

Supporting 3PLs and sellers with standardized packaging SOPs.

Dispatch Scheduling

Coordinating dispatch windows with carriers.

Shipment Coordination & Tracking

Providing real-time visibility and communication across the supply chain.

Shipment Tracking

Live tracking updates shared via SMS, email, chat, or WhatsApp.

Last-Mile Coordination

Resolving issues during final delivery (incorrect address, delivery slot change, etc.).

Carrier Communication

Acting as a liaison between marketplace, 3PL, and delivery agents.

Reverse Logistics

Handling return pickups, transit damage claims, and restocking.

Route Optimization Support

Analyzing data to suggest more efficient delivery routes.

Buyer Support

Improving customer satisfaction through proactive and responsive logistics communication.

Order Status Updates

Notifying buyers at every stage processed, shipped, out for delivery, delivered.

Delivery Rescheduling

Managing failed delivery attempts or buyer-requested changes.

Lost/Damaged Item Claims

Filing claims and coordinating replacements/refunds.

Address Validation & Correction

Assisting buyers in correcting wrong/incomplete addresses.

Feedback & NPS Collection

Capturing logistics-related CX data post-delivery.

Seller & Warehouse Support

Helping sellers ensure timely and accurate order dispatch.

AWB Generation & Manifesting

Pickup Scheduling

Packaging SOP Compliance

Ensuring correct labelling and packaging to avoid penalties.

Non-Delivery Reconciliation

Tracking and reconciling RTO (Return to Origin) and undelivered shipments.

Performance Reporting

Daily/weekly reports on TAT, SLA breaches, and exceptions.

Carrier/Vendor Support

Onboarding New Carriers

Invoicing & Payment Support
